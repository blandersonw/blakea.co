Lessons I will teach my kids.

1.

Throughout your childhood, I will teach you everything that has served me well. Many lessons that applied to me will not apply to you. You can learn a lot from others' experiences, but always take it with a grain of salt until you know it to be true through your own.

2.

Four pillars of personal improvement: nutrition, exercise, purpose, and rest.

3.

There's nothing wrong with a transactional relationship, but don't treat people transactionally by default - expecially loved ones.

4.

Brave enough to put it all on the line, intelligent enough to know when not to.

5.

Speak the truth, no matter how it is received. Act altruistically and all will proceed as it should.

6.

Do not be anxious, for nobody thinks about you as much as yourself and everybody dies in the end.

7.

Surrender yourself.
Open your heart and your eyes.
Smell the roses.
